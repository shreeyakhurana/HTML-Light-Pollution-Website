Lighting Schemes

Lighting schemes are the types of light fixtures in a neighborhood.

Full Cut Off lamp

The full cut off lamp directs light downwards and cuts the light off so it can't go upwards.

Masked Lamp

The Masked lamp is masked from the top, blocking light from escaping upwards. Yet, it still allows light to escape sideways.

Unshielded Lamp

The Unshielded lamp is completely un-covered allowing light to escape in all directions.
