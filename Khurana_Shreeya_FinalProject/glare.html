Glare

Glare is objectionable brightness or the sensation produced by luminance
within the visual field that is sufficiently greater than the luminance to
which the eyes are adapted to cause annoyance, discomfort, or loss in visual performance and visibility.
Glare can be extremely harmful and can cause accidents with humans and animals alike - car accidents mainly.


Can you see anything but lights right into your eyes? If so, good for you. If not, there is supposed to be a stop sign in front of you.
