The Effect on Animals

Light pollution not only affects us humans negatively but it also has been shown to have harmful effects on plants and animals.
For example, newborn sea turtles move towards light, which, for them, is the reflection of the moon on the sea. Because of this,
hundreds of thousands of sea turtles are lost every year because they are attracted by the bright lights of the city, thinking that it is the moon.
In 2008, hundreds of migratory birds were found dead outside a Tucker County High School. These birds became disoriented from the fog and lighting
around the school causing them to fly into structures.
