Skyglow

Skyglow is another type of light pollution.
It is the illumination of the night sky or parts of it.
Skyglow is measured in lumens, and the higher the skyglow, the better.
For example, the stars will be brighter - based on observation - in locations of skyglow 20, than in locations of skyglow 16.
